Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How accurate are the predictions?
A: Our app uses advanced statistical analysis and machine learning algorithms to identify patterns in historical data. However, lottery numbers are inherently random, and we cannot guarantee winning results. The app is designed for entertainment and educational purposes.
Q: What is the difference between free and premium features?
A: Free features include basic number analysis and pattern recognition. Premium features ($0.99/month or $9.99/year) unlock Five-Algorithm Analysis, Machine Learning Prediction, unlimited predictions, and advanced backtest validation.
Q: How often is the data updated?
A: We update Powerball drawing data automatically after each official draw. The app checks for new data every hour and maintains a comprehensive historical database for analysis.
Q: Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
A: Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time through your device's App Store settings. Your premium features will remain active until the end of your current billing period.
Q: Does the app work offline?
A: Yes, once data is downloaded, you can perform analysis offline. However, you'll need an internet connection to download the latest drawing results and verify your subscription status.
Q: What iOS version is required?
A: Powerball Analyzer requires iOS 18.0 or later. The app is optimized for iPhone and iPad devices.

How to Use the App

Getting Started

  • Download and install Powerball Analyzer from the App Store
  • Open the app and wait for the initial data download
  • Explore free features like Number Analysis and Pattern Recognition
  • Consider upgrading to Premium for advanced AI features

Understanding Analysis Results

  • Hot Numbers: Numbers that appear more frequently than average
  • Cold Numbers: Numbers that appear less frequently than average
  • Accuracy Rate: Percentage of successful predictions in backtesting
  • Confidence Level: Algorithm's confidence in the prediction (15%-95%)

Premium Features Guide

  • Five-Algorithm Analysis: Click on algorithm cards to calculate individual predictions
  • Machine Learning: Select training data range (50-200 periods) for AI analysis
  • Backtest Results: Review historical performance metrics and ROI calculations

Troubleshooting

App Crashes or Freezes

  • Force close the app and restart it
  • Restart your device
  • Ensure you have sufficient storage space (at least 100MB free)
  • Update to the latest version from the App Store

Data Loading Issues

  • Check your internet connection
  • Try the "Reload" button on the main screen
  • Close and reopen the app
  • Ensure you're not using a VPN that might block data requests

Subscription Problems

  • Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions to manage your subscription
  • Use "Restore Purchases" in the app if premium features aren't available
  • Ensure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for purchase
  • Contact Apple Support for billing issues

Performance Issues

  • Machine Learning calculations are CPU-intensive and may take 30-60 seconds
  • Close other apps to free up memory
  • Use shorter training periods (50 periods) for faster results
  • Avoid running multiple analyses simultaneously

System Requirements

  • iOS: 18.0 or later
  • Device: iPhone or iPad
  • Storage: 50MB minimum, 200MB recommended
  • Internet: Required for data updates and subscription verification
  • Processor: A12 Bionic or newer recommended for optimal performance
